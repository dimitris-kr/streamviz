StreamViz

Streaming Platform Visual Comparisons

Here you'll find interactive data visualizations that analyse and compare the content (movies & tv), subscribers and revenue of the most popular streaming platforms today!

The Idea

The streaming era has revolutionized the way people access movies, series etc. as well as the way the entertainment business works. This project offers data visualizations concerning the content, revenue, and subscribers of various streaming platforms, to acquire knowledge regarding the differences in what they offer, possible trends, and their growth over time.

Streaming Nowadays

Streaming platforms are becoming increasingly popular nowadays. People are able to choose from an abundant catalogue of movies and series and watch whenever and wherever they desire. Therefore, due to this great convenience and a low cost, streaming is becoming the standard way of consuming media. That's why we chose to analyse this phenomenon through data visualization.

flag

The Goal of this Project

The aim of this project is to extract information, through the visualizations, regarding which streaming platform outperforms the others in terms of overall success, quantified by number of users and revenue. In addition, we attempt to find possible relations between movie/series characteristics, for instance whether the year that a movie was released or its genre affects its rating, and how it is manifests for each platform.
