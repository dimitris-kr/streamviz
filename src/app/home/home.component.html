StreamViz

Streaming Platforms & User Preference

Here you'll find interactive data visualizations that analyse and compare the content (movies & tv), subscribers and revenue of the most popular streaming platforms today!

The Idea

The streaming era has revolutionized the way people access movies, series etc. as well as the way the entertainment business works. This project offers data visualizations concerning the content, revenue, and subscribers of various streaming platforms, to acquire knowledge regarding the differences in what they offer, possible trends, and their growth over time.
